# QRCode_
🔳 QRify – Smart QR Code Generator

QRify is a modern Android application that allows users to generate custom QR codes from multiple input types such as text, email, phone number, website URLs, images, and more.
The app focuses on simplicity, customization, and clean design while supporting multiple QR templates.

📱 About the App

QRify makes QR code creation fast, flexible, and user-friendly.

Users can generate QR codes from:

Plain text

Email addresses

Phone numbers

Website URLs

Images & files

Custom formatted templates

Each QR code can be customized using different design templates, colors, and styles, making QRify suitable for both personal and professional use.

✨ Key Features

🔤 Generate QR from Text

📧 QR for Email

📞 QR for Phone Number

🌐 QR for Web Links

🖼️ QR from Images

🎨 Multiple QR Templates & Styles

📐 Custom colors & shapes

💾 Save QR codes locally

🔗 Share QR codes instantly

📤 Export as image

🛠️ Tech Stack

⚙️ Kotlin – Native Android development

🎨 Canvas / Custom Views – QR rendering & templates

🧩 Jetpack Components – ViewModel, LiveData

🗄️ Room Database – Save QR history

🔥 Firebase – Analytics / optional cloud sync

📷 ZXing / ML Kit – QR code generation & processing

🧱 Architecture

QRify follows modern Android architecture:

MVVM (Model–View–ViewModel)

Clean separation of UI and logic

Reusable QR templates

Offline-first QR generation

Scalable & maintainable codebase

🎯 Use Cases

Business cards & contact sharing

Marketing campaigns

Event invitations

Wi-Fi sharing

Educational & office use

Portfolio & demo projects

🔒 Privacy

No personal data is shared

QR generation works fully offline

Images and files remain on the device unless shared by the user
